About BentaMatic

BentaMatic Online Store is your one-stop shop for affordable appliances. We offer a wide range of high-quality appliances from trusted brands, all at competitive prices.

At BentaMatic, we understand that purchasing appliances can be a significant investment. That's why we provide flexible financing options to our customers. Our loan services allow you to finance your purchases conveniently and affordably.

We have our main office located in BGC Taguig, and we have 32 physical stores nationwide. You can choose to have your orders delivered directly to your doorstep, or you can opt for pickup at any of our physical stores for added convenience.

Our nationwide delivery service ensures that customers from all corners of the country can enjoy the convenience of shopping with BentaMatic. No matter where you are, we'll make sure your appliances are delivered safely and efficiently.
